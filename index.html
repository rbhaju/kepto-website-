Smart Scan, powered by Apple Intelligence

Your phone reads the receipt. Nothing leaves it.

On iPhone 15 Pro and newer with Apple Intelligence on, Kepto uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework to extract fields from a scanned receipt in seconds. Older iPhones use a local heuristic parser. Either way, the image, the OCR text, and the parsed fields all stay on your device.

100% on device. Nothing is ever sent anywhere.

Tax and GST tracking

Tax tracked, on every purchase.

Kepto records the tax paid on every receipt, either manually or pre-filled at scan time. The Tax row sits right under Price on the form, so you can see it the moment a scan lands and correct anything that doesn't look right. At tax time, every receipt's GST is already there, ready to export.

Calm warranty reminders

A gentle nudge before your warranty ends.

Kepto schedules a calm local notification a few weeks before each warranty expires, so you never miss a claim window. No spam, no daily reminders, no marketing. Just a heads-up when it matters.

Backup and restore

Yours forever, on any device.

Export every receipt, every photo, and every custom category to a single file you can save anywhere — iCloud Drive, Files, AirDrop, email. Restore on a new phone in one tap. Free for everyone.

Your data never leaves your phone.

No analytics. No advertising SDKs. No tracking. No accounts. No servers. Kepto has nothing to send and nothing to sell.
