Kepto Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2026

Kepto is built on one principle: your data stays with you. Here's exactly how your information is handled, section by section.

Your data stays with you

Kepto stores all your warranty and receipt information locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is uploaded to the developer's servers.

iCloud sync

If you're signed into iCloud, your items are also synced to your private iCloud account and shared across the Apple devices using the same iCloud login. The developer of Kepto never sees or stores this data. It moves directly between your device and Apple's iCloud servers.

Receipt scanning and storage

When you scan or upload a receipt, the image is processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Neither the image nor the extracted text is ever sent over the network. The original receipt photo is saved locally alongside the item record so you can view it later.

Kepto Premium

Premium unlocks unlimited items, PDF and CSV exports, date range exports, service reminders, advanced stats and custom categories as a one-time purchase. The purchase is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. Kepto receives only a yes-or-no entitlement and a transaction ID. Retriever Labs never sees your Apple ID, payment details, or location. You can restore a previous purchase any time from the upgrade screen.

Exporting your data

Kepto can create several kinds of export, each handed straight to the system share sheet. Anything you do with the resulting file (AirDrop, email, save to Files, and so on) is your choice.

  • PDF Archive — a formatted document of all your items.
  • CSV Spreadsheet — a file containing all your item details.
  • Date Range PDF (Premium) — a PDF limited to the date range you choose.
  • Single-item PDF — share an individual item as a PDF from its detail screen.

Notifications

Kepto schedules local notifications on your device only. No notification content is ever sent over the network. This covers:

  • Warranty reminders — alerts before an item's warranty expires.
  • Monthly check-in — a reminder on the 1st of each month.
  • Service reminders (Premium) — per-item reminders, off by default.

You can turn any of these off at any time in Settings.

No analytics

Kepto does not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any other telemetry.
